What we Offer?

Our NGO is dedicated to offering support and resources for those in situations of inequality and poverty. We offer a variety of services to support people in situations of poverty and inequality. Our programs include:

  1. Food and housing assistance: We provide food aid and assistance to ensure that people have access to nutritious food and a roof over their heads. We work in collaboration with local organizations to distribute food and offer support to find affordable housing.
  2. Medical care and health services: We ensure that all people have access to quality medical care, regardless of their ability to pay. We organize free health clinics and provide assistance to access necessary medications and treatments.
  3. Education and training programs: We believe in the transformative power of education to break the cycle of poverty. We offer educational and vocational training programs to help people acquire new skills and increase their employment opportunities and economic progress.
  4. Psychosocial and emotional support: We recognize that poverty and inequality not only affect people materially, but also emotionally and psychologically. That's why we offer psychosocial and emotional support services to help people face challenges and overcome the barriers they face.
  5. Advocacy for rights and promotion of public policies: We are committed to advocating for public policies that address the underlying causes of inequality and poverty in Spain. We work to raise awareness and mobilize the community in the fight for social and economic justice.
  6. Microcredits and financing: Providing access to microcredits and financing for entrepreneurs and small businesses in disadvantaged communities, helping them start or expand their businesses and generate sustainable income.
  7. Employment insertion programs: Offering employment insertion programs that include training, career guidance, and job placement for unemployed or socially excluded people, facilitating their integration into the labor market.
  8. Legal services and counseling: Providing free or low-cost legal services, as well as legal advice, for people facing legal issues related to housing, employment, migration, or other aspects of their lives.
  9. Access to basic services: Facilitating access to basic services such as clean water, electricity, public transportation, and telecommunications in marginalized or rural communities lacking these essential services.
  10. Promotion of gender equality: Developing programs and awareness campaigns that promote gender equality and empower women and girls, providing resources and support to overcome the barriers they face in education, employment, and political participation.
  11. Promotion of social inclusion: Organizing community activities and events that promote integration and social cohesion, encouraging participation and dialogue among different ethnic, cultural, and socioeconomic groups.
  12. Research and political advocacy: Conducting research and analysis on the causes and consequences of inequality and poverty in Spain, and advocating for more inclusive and equitable public policies at the local, regional, and national levels.
